DHOKRA

Dhokra is a traditional Indian metal casting technique that dates back over 4,000 years. It is known for its exquisite and intricate handmade metalwork, created using the lost-wax casting method. Dhokra handicrafts are crafted by skilled artisans using a blend of beeswax, clay, and straw, which is then covered with a layer of clay and heated to produce unique and rustic metal sculptures, jewelry, and home decor items . With their timeless appeal and cultural significance, Dhokra handicrafts embody the rich heritage and artistic traditions of India, making them a perfect choice for those seeking to add an authentic touch to their living spaces or unique accessories to their personal style.
