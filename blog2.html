Kanchipuram saris

The term Kanjivaram Saree is also known as Kanjeevaram Sarees, Kanchi Cotton Sarees, and Kanchipuram Silk Sarees. As the name sounds, it came from a town in Tamil Nadu called Kanchipuram where the Saree was originally originated. History show, the Chola King Krishna Deva Raya who ruled the Kanchipuram city from the year 985 to 1015, takes the initiative of trading silk sarees. The real weaving experts the Devangas and the Saligars visited the city Kanchipuram at that time to boost the silk trading. history show, the Chola King Krishna Deva Raya who ruled the Kanchipuram city from the year 985 to 1015, takes the initiative of trading silk sarees.
